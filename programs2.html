We welcome all female-identifying undergraduate students to apply for our mentorship program where we match mentees to student mentors based on personality, goals, and preferences. This mentorship program provides an opportunity to build a 1-1 connection with an experienced mentor in a S.T.E.A.M (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics) or Finance field.

mentor

An undergraduate female who is majoring in S.T.E.A.M or Finance, is looking to give back to their community, and has been part of at least one externship, internship, or official program


apply now

mentee

An undergraduate female in a S.T.E.A.M or Finance major looking for mentorship from an upperclassman to help guide them on their professional journey.
