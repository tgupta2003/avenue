As NYU Freshman enrolled in STEAM classes, we quickly identified a gap in support resources for women. After interviewing 25 undergraduate women majoring in CS, attending career-orientated panels, school clubs and via personal experience, we identified this critical need for support and went on to create avenue...

avenue is a non-profit striving to build community for STEAM+Finance female undergraduates who want to foster meaningful relationships, find a support system, and broaden their network.
